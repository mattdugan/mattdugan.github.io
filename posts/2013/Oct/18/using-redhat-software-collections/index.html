DwD E-001: Using Red Hat Software Collections

Welcome to our first episode of Deep with Dugan. This ongoing series will provide short in depth looks at the technologies we leverage at Shadow Soft and through our partners. If something strikes us in the industry, you might see it here as well. This first episode explores using Red Hat Software Collections for modern application development.

The first obstacle many people encounter when using Red Hat Enterprise Linux as their enterprise development platform is the apparent lack of the modern languages and tools they are accustomed to using. For example, Python 3, PHP 5, or Ruby on Rails or even the 5.2 release of PostgreSQL.
